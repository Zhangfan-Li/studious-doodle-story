There is a student in UW Madison, whose name is Jack. He has a lot of work to do everyday. His only hobby is playing video game. One day after finishing a lot of homework, he started the game. Suddenly, he found himself enter the world of that game. He became a great general. He has a large amount of soldiers. He thought, "What should I do now?". He became hungry for more power and wealth. He started attacking and looting peaceful places. The people of the video game became angry with the way Jack abused his power. Even though they were just robots, they still were able to feel emotion. To help bring peace back to the world game, they decided to plan a secret attack that would stop Jack's reign once and for all.
